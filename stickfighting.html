Stick Fighting

  Stick fighting is probably what Filipino martial arts are best know for. Practitioners utilise rattan sticks for training purposes. Training invloves solo technique training, and partner drills

Techniques of Stick Fighting
  • Striking
  • Locking
  • Disarming
  • Blocking
